Introduction

Hello my name is Elizabeth, and welcome to my portfolio! I wanted to create a place for you to view my work, comment, ask for a uniquely designed piece, or learn more about how I got started.

My Start

It all started when I was a little girl crafting with paints, fabric, pipe cleaners, and toilet paper rolls. I loved being crafty but I came across a whole world of things you can create with the fabric medium. I had my first sewing machine when I was 10 years old. I taught myself how to sew my own clothes through blogs, portfolios, and Youtube. From there I got a serging machine, and became in my eyes unstoppable in the sewing world. WHile I loved the design world I also sprouted a love for code which is why I now sew as a hobby.

My Art Today

I still sew clothing, handbags, and other accesories for fun. If you are interested in owning a custom piece please feel free to reach out on my "Order a Custom Piece" page! If you are new and would like to see what kind of work I do take a look at my "Portfolio" page!
