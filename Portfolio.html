My Creations
This was the purse I made my niece for Christmas. She has never had a purse before, and her favorite color is purple. I filled it with homemade scrunchies, and girly things such as lip gloss and nail polish.
This is the bag that I made for my sister for Christmas. She enjoys very large bags as she is a mom of two and has to be prepared for every situation. There are 5 different pockets inside of varying sizes. It is lined in a red satin and the outside is a faux leather. I did make another for my other sister with a crushed pink velvet lining and adjustable straps.
These are joggers that I made to lounge around the house during the pandemic. They have an elastic waistband, and pockets.
During the fall season I noticed that snake skin print was coming back into style and decided to create this top. I tend to tuck in my shirts into a pair of jeans for most of my looks, so I decided to make this into a bodysuit that way I don't have to worry about tucking it.
